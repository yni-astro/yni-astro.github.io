I am a PhD candidate in astronomy at Tsinghua University, advised by Prof. Xuening Bai. My research uses (radiation-)hydrodynamic simulations to study how stars and planets are born — from the giant molecular clouds that birth star clusters, all the way down to protoplanetary disks where gas giants emerge.

Currently, I focus on the early-stage evolution of self-gravitating protoplanetary disks and on the life cycle of giant molecular clouds in galaxy-scale simulations. I am also a developer of the M1-radiative transfer module in the GIZMO code.
